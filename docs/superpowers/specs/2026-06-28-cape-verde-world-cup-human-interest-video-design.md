# Cape Verde World Cup Human Interest Video Design

## Goal

Create a 5-8 minute horizontal 16:9 Chinese video about Cabo Verde, built in HyperFrames from sourced external video clips plus animated editorial graphics.

The video uses the current 2026 World Cup moment as the hook: Cabo Verde have reached the Round of 32 and will face defending champions Argentina. From there, the video expands into a national profile: geography, culture, development, football growth, and the global diaspora behind the Blue Sharks.

The tone should be:

- Opening: hot-blooded, slightly over-the-top, meme-friendly.
- Middle: cinematic sports-humanities documentary.
- Ending: emotional and elevated, returning to the World Cup challenge.

Target output:

- Format: 16:9 horizontal.
- Duration: about 6 minutes, acceptable range 5-8 minutes.
- Language: Chinese narration and burned-in Chinese subtitles.
- Voice: fluent male Chinese voice, medium pace; opening and football sections more energetic, country/culture sections calmer.

## Approved Creative Direction

Working title:

> 佛的脚下凡，挑战世界冠军

Opening hook:

> 佛的脚，真的来人间比赛了。一个大西洋上的群岛国家，第一次杀进世界杯淘汰赛。下一站，挑战世界冠军阿根廷。

Core thesis:

> This is not just a football underdog story. It is a story about a small Atlantic island country, a huge diaspora, and a national team that turned geography, migration, and identity into a World Cup miracle.

Closing idea:

> 佛的脚，不一定能踢翻世界冠军。但它已经告诉世界：地图上最小的名字，也能走到最大的球场中央。

## Narrative Structure

### 1. Cold Open: 佛的脚下凡, 0:00-0:35

Purpose:

- Stop the scroll immediately.
- Turn the country name into a memorable hook without making the whole video feel cheap.
- Establish the sporting stakes: Cabo Verde vs Argentina.

Visuals:

- Dark stadium lights.
- Cabo Verde flag flashes.
- Atlantic map zoom from world map to West Africa to the islands.
- Big kinetic typography: "佛的脚", "下凡", "挑战阿根廷".
- Quick Round of 32 bracket reveal.

Narration notes:

- Fast, punchy, meme-aware.
- Say clearly that "佛得角" is a Chinese name pun; do not imply a religious claim.
- Move quickly from joke to real achievement.

### 2. Where Is This Country?, 0:35-1:35

Purpose:

- Make viewers understand Cabo Verde geographically.
- Reframe it from "unknown name" to "Atlantic crossroads".

Facts to include:

- It is an archipelago in the Atlantic, off the west coast of Africa.
- Ten main islands, with nine inhabited; official tourism messaging emphasizes ten islands.
- Praia is the capital on Santiago.
- Fogo, Santo Antao, Sao Vicente, Sal, Boa Vista, and Santiago provide distinct visual identities.

Visuals:

- Map labels.
- Island-to-island line animation.
- Drone footage: coastlines, volcano, beaches, city streets.
- Chapter card: "不是一个角，是一片群岛".

### 3. Culture And Personality, 1:35-2:45

Purpose:

- Explain why Cabo Verde feels like an Atlantic mix rather than a simple African mainland story.
- Give the middle section texture and humanity.

Facts to include:

- Portuguese is the official language; Cabo Verdean Creole / Kriolu is central to everyday identity.
- Culture blends African, Portuguese, and Atlantic diaspora influences.
- Morna is a signature musical practice and is listed by UNESCO as intangible cultural heritage.
- Sao Vicente and Mindelo are useful culture/music visual anchors.
- "Morabeza" can be used as a cultural keyword: warmth, hospitality, island openness.

Visuals:

- Morna performance or music-related footage.
- Mindelo streets, port, plazas, people, seaside.
- Warm lower-thirds with cultural keywords.

Tone:

- Slow the narration slightly.
- Let music breathe for a few seconds if the footage supports it.

### 4. Development Reality, 2:45-3:45

Purpose:

- Avoid turning the country into only beaches or only football.
- Explain the development contradiction: beautiful islands, limited resources, service/tourism economy, climate and water pressure.

Facts to include:

- Tourism is a major driver of the economy; the World Bank notes it around a quarter of GDP.
- Arable land is limited, and mineral resources are scarce.
- Remittances and diaspora links matter.
- The country is often described as politically stable and service-oriented, but it remains exposed to tourism shocks, drought, imports, and climate risk.

Visuals:

- Tourism islands: Sal, Boa Vista.
- Port, airport, hotel, road, market.
- Dry land, volcanic terrain, sea.
- Animated "development dashboard": tourism, remittances, services, water pressure.

Tone:

- Balanced. Do not romanticize poverty.
- Use simple data, not too many numbers.

### 5. Why Football Exploded, 3:45-5:20

Purpose:

- Make the football success feel earned.
- Connect beach football, diaspora, European club pathways, and national identity.

Facts and story beats:

- Cabo Verde's small population makes this run feel disproportionate.
- The Blue Sharks draw strength from players developed across Europe and the diaspora.
- The team has been building credibility through AFCON appearances and World Cup qualifying before this knockout run.
- Pico Lopes is a strong example of diaspora scouting and unusual pathways.
- Vozinha can be used as a veteran goalkeeper angle if sourced carefully.
- Football functions as a shared language between islands and diaspora.

Visuals:

- Training, fans, players walking out, team celebration.
- Data cards: "人口小国", "旅欧球员", "蓝鲨队", "三场平局出线".
- Blue shark motif, but keep it graphic and restrained.
- World map lines from Cabo Verde to Portugal, Netherlands, Ireland, France, and other diaspora/club centers.

Tone:

- Rise again into sports energy.
- The line should be: small country, big network, fierce identity.

### 6. Challenge Argentina, 5:20-6:10

Purpose:

- Bring the story back to the hook and leave viewers with emotional stakes.

Facts to include:

- Round of 32: Argentina vs Cabo Verde.
- Argentina are defending champions and a global football giant.
- Cabo Verde do not need to be framed as favorites; the miracle is already that they are there.

Visuals:

- Bracket board: ARG vs CPV.
- Argentina represented by blue-white champion graphics, not necessarily copyrighted match footage.
- Cabo Verde flag and blue shark line.
- Final wide shot: ocean, stadium lights, flag.

Ending line:

> 佛的脚，不一定能踢翻世界冠军。但它已经告诉世界：地图上最小的名字，也能走到最大的球场中央。

## Script Style

Rules:

- Use hot-blooded sports Chinese, but do not imitate any real commentator.
- Do not keep the entire video in "中二" mode. Use the hook, then earn the documentary tone.
- Use short sentences in opening and football sections.
- Use slightly longer, smoother sentences in geography/culture/development sections.
- Avoid dense history lectures.
- Avoid unsupported claims about poverty, politics, injuries, or player availability.
- Name the country consistently as "佛得角 / Cabo Verde" early, then mostly "佛得角".
- Use "蓝鲨队" as the recurring football image.

Example narration flow:

```text
佛的脚，真的来人间比赛了。
不是段子。
这个大西洋上的群岛国家，第一次站上世界杯淘汰赛。
而它下一场要挑战的，是世界冠军阿根廷。

但如果你只把它当成一个冷门比分，那就太小看这个故事了。
佛得角不是突然被世界杯照亮。
它本来就是大西洋上，一串被海风、迁徙、音乐和足球连接起来的岛。
```

## Visual Identity

Create a local `DESIGN.md` in the HyperFrames project before writing composition HTML.

Style name:

> Atlantic Epic Sports Dossier

Mood:

- Cinematic.
- Oceanic.
- Urgent in opening and ending.
- Warm and documentary-like in the middle.

Palette:

- Atlantic navy: `#061B2E`
- Deep ocean: `#0B3558`
- Volcanic black: `#111217`
- Cabo Verde blue: `#1A4FB3`
- Flag red: `#D71920`
- Flag yellow: `#F7D117`
- Warm white: `#F6F2E8`
- Data gold: `#E7B84A`

Typography:

- Chinese: `Noto Sans SC` or `Source Han Sans SC`.
- Latin/numeric: `Inter`.
- Opening title can use heavier weight, but keep captions readable.

Motion:

- Opening: smash cuts, fast map zoom, flag color wipes, title scale hits.
- Geography: map lines draw between islands.
- Culture: softer dissolves, slow parallax, warm lower-thirds.
- Development: dashboard cards slide in cleanly.
- Football: bracket lines, player cards, route maps, blue shark trace.
- Transitions: use ocean-wave wipes, pitch-line reveals, and flag-color slashes.

Avoid:

- Purple/blue tech gradients.
- Decorative orbs or bokeh.
- Long paragraphs on screen.
- Shark cartoon mascots that make the topic childish.
- Overusing the "佛的脚" joke after the opening.

## Risks And Mitigations

### Current World Cup facts may keep changing

Mitigation:

- Timestamp the script as "截至 2026年6月28日".
- Re-check Round of 32 schedule and opponent details immediately before rendering.

### YouTube clips may not be downloadable

Mitigation:

- Use multiple candidates per function: geography, culture, football, fans.
- Keep HyperFrames graphics strong enough to carry sections without exact footage.
- Ask the user to provide downloaded files if needed.

### Copyright and platform risk

Mitigation:

- Use short, transformed excerpts for commentary and analysis.
- Avoid long unmodified match broadcasts.
- Prefer official tourism, documentary, or public informational clips when possible.
- Use graphics for Argentina/World Cup bracket rather than extensive match footage.

### Tone could become too silly

Mitigation:

- Use the pun only as the opening trigger.
- Shift to documentary authority after the first 20-30 seconds.
- End with emotional dignity, not another joke.

### Geography/culture section could feel slow

Mitigation:

- Keep each non-football section visually active with maps, chapter cards, and data chips.
- Return to the World Cup question at every chapter transition.
