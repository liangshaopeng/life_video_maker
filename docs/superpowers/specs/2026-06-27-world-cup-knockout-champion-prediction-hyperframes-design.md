# 2026 World Cup Knockout And Champion Prediction HyperFrames Design

## Goal

Create a 4-minute vertical Chinese口播 video for the state of the 2026 FIFA World Cup at **2026-06-27 18:00 Beijing time**.

The video should explain:

- Which teams have already qualified or are close to qualification.
- How the Round of 32 currently projects.
- Which contenders have the cleanest or most dangerous routes.
- Why the champion pick is France, while Portugal and Argentina still have clear but difficult title paths.

The output should be a HyperFrames project, not the older real-footage pipeline. It should use animated tables, bracket lines, contender cards, flag marks, probability bars, and synchronized Chinese subtitles. No copyrighted match footage is required.

## Editorial Thesis

The video should sound decisive:

> "现在预测冠军，不能只看名气。要看三件事：小组状态、淘汰赛签位、核心球员是不是已经进入杀人模式。按这三个维度，目前法国是最完整的冠军答案。"

France is the champion pick because:

- They finished the group with the strongest major-contender profile: 3 wins, 10 goals, 2 conceded.
- Their attack has multiple live weapons: Mbappe and Dembele are both in the Golden Boot race.
- Their projected route is hard but legible: Sweden, then likely Germany, then a heavyweight quarterfinal. If they pass Germany, their title credibility becomes the strongest in the field.

Argentina and Portugal should not be dismissed. They become the two "if they solve this, they can win it" teams.

## Required Portugal Segment

Portugal's title path is not about whether Cristiano Ronaldo can still score. He already has 2 goals and the Uzbekistan match showed he can still punish weaker opponents.

The real championship questions:

- **First, beat Colombia if possible.** Portugal are qualified, but if they stay second in Group K, the current projection sends them to Ghana and then likely Spain. That is a brutal tax before the tournament has even opened up.
- **Second, solve the Ronaldo role.** Ronaldo can still finish chances, but Portugal cannot let the entire pressing structure and transition defense bend around a 41-year-old striker for 120-minute knockout games.
- **Third, protect the rest defense.** A Bruno/Vitinha/Leao-style attacking unit is beautiful, but if the fullbacks push together and the midfield loses the second ball, Brazil, Spain, France, or Argentina can punish the space behind them.
- **Fourth, let the golden generation own the game.** Portugal's depth is the reason they can win; Ronaldo should be the emotional weapon and box weapon, not the only idea.

Editorial line:

> "葡萄牙要夺冠，第一步不是喊最后一舞，而是先把签位抢回来；第二步，是让C罗成为终结点，而不是整支球队的重心。"

## Required Argentina Segment

Argentina have the cleanest early road among the biggest contenders: current projection gives them Cape Verde in the Round of 32, then a manageable Round of 16 lane against Australia or Egypt.

The real championship questions:

- **First, manage Messi's minutes.** Messi leads the Golden Boot race with 5 goals, but a 39-year-old核心 cannot be asked to carry every press, every transition, and every late-game escape.
- **Second, prove the non-Messi attack is real.** If opponents double Messi and slow the tempo, Argentina need Alvarez, Lautaro, Enzo, Mac Allister, or wide runners to produce goals without waiting for magic.
- **Third, survive the quarterfinal fork.** If Portugal win Group K, Argentina's quarterfinal path could become a Messi vs Ronaldo chapter. If Colombia hold first, it may be a different kind of South American duel. Either way, the bracket stops being gentle around the quarterfinal.
- **Fourth, keep emotional control.** Argentina are champions, but defending a title means every opponent turns the match into an event. Yellow cards, extra time, and shootout pressure can drain them before the final.

Editorial line:

> "阿根廷的签位一开始很好，但他们真正要克服的是依赖。梅西越神，越要有人替他分担。否则走到八强、四强，魔法会被体能账单追上。"

## Video Structure

Target duration: 235-250 seconds.

1. **Cold open, 0-20s**
   - Hook: "现在的世界杯，已经不是小组赛了，是冠军路线图。"
   - Show a pulsing timestamp: "北京时间 6月27日 18:00".
   - Visual: world map/stadium grid, groups A-L lighting up.

2. **Group scan, 20-65s**
   - Fast scan of confirmed qualifiers: Mexico, Switzerland, Brazil, USA, Germany, Netherlands, Belgium, Spain, France, Argentina, plus runners-up and best third-place teams.
   - Call out the unresolved but decisive groups: J/K/L.
   - Visual: 12 mini group tables, settled groups locked, J/K/L in amber "待落位".

3. **Round of 32 map, 65-115s**
   - Highlight current projections: Brazil-Japan, Germany-Paraguay, Netherlands-Morocco, France-Sweden, Spain-Austria, Portugal-Ghana, Argentina-Cape Verde.
   - Make clear that J/K/L can still affect exact routes.
   - Visual: bracket branches animate from left to right.

4. **Contender truth table, 115-180s**
   - France: best current champion pick.
   - Argentina: great early road, Messi management problem.
   - Portugal: title talent, but must fix route and structure.
   - Brazil/Spain/Germany/Netherlands: serious contenders but with sharper flaws or harder matchups.
   - Visual: contender cards with "状态", "签位", "隐患", "夺冠温度".

5. **Portugal and Argentina deep beat, 180-220s**
   - Portugal: must beat Colombia for first place; if second, Ghana then likely Spain. Need Ronaldo as finisher, not system center.
   - Argentina: must protect Messi, diversify attack, and survive a possible Portugal/Colombia quarterfinal fork.
   - Visual: split-screen "C罗问题" vs "梅西账单".

6. **Champion prediction, 220-250s**
   - Prediction: France champion.
   - Final four prediction: France, Spain/Portugal, Argentina, Brazil/Mexico-side winner. Keep exact J/K/L branch caveated.
   - Closing line: "我押法国，不是因为他们名气最大，而是因为到现在为止，他们的状态、厚度和杀伤点最完整。"

## Script Style

The voice should be Chinese sports-commentary口播, but not impersonating any real commentator.

Rules:

- Use "朋友们" sparingly, no more than 3 times.
- Use short sentences and punchy transitions.
- Avoid unsupported injury claims unless cited in the final data notes.
- Say "预测" and "如果现在开打" when the bracket depends on unfinished groups.
- Keep team and player names intact in captions: "C罗", "梅西", "姆巴佩", "登贝莱", "维尼修斯", "哈兰德", "B费", "维蒂尼亚".

## Visual Identity

Create a local `DESIGN.md` for the HyperFrames project.

Style:

- Mood: cinematic, analytical, urgent.
- Canvas: dark stadium board, not a generic gradient.
- Main colors:
  - pitch black: `#07110D`
  - line green: `#37D67A`
  - trophy gold: `#F6C64A`
  - alert red: `#F04444`
  - text white: `#F5F7EF`
  - slate panel: `#14201A`
- Typography:
  - Chinese display: `Noto Sans SC` or `Source Han Sans SC`
  - Latin/numeric: `Inter`
- Motion:
  - Bracket lines draw on like a tactical board.
  - Cards enter with staggered y/opacity and small scale.
  - Scene transitions use wipes or pitch-line reveals.

Avoid:

- Purple/blue tech gradients.
- Decorative orbs or bokeh blobs.
- Long paragraphs on screen.
- In-app explainer text about how to use the video.

## Risks

- The exact bracket can shift after the Group J/K/L matches later on June 27 in North America.
  - Mitigation: timestamp the video and phrase those sections as current projections.

- Third-place permutations can change current Round of 32 opponents.
  - Mitigation: show "if it started now" and avoid pretending the bracket is final where it is not.

- HyperFrames TTS availability may depend on local setup.
  - Mitigation: author the script and captions deterministically, then use the CLI TTS path or a local fallback only if needed.
